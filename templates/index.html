What is this?

World Wide Waiting Room (WWWR) is a multiplayer idle game about accumulating time.

While you are on the site, you will passively accumulate wait time.

However, when you go offline you will also accumulate time. The longer you stay offline, the more vulnerable you become.

Other players who are online can, once per day, steal an amount of wait time from offline players equal to the amount of time that player has been offline. Waitime cannot go in the negative. If an offline player has no time to give, then they are removed from the offline queue.

Your objective is to be the longest waiter in the queue when the game timer ends. At which point the game state will be saved, placed on the global leaderboard, and a new game will begin.
